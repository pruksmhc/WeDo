Welcome

WeDo - the next step to productivity.

Bringing the power of focus groups to the web.

After signing up for a free trial of WeDo, you can add up to two projects that you would like to work on. After tagging your product and creating some initial to-dos, explore the projects that other people are doing. Find someone or a project that you like? Message that person, and if you are a good match, merge to-do lists! From there, you will get a mail notification (or push notification) when the other person completes a to-do item in tehir list. See the other is slacking? Help motivate the other person by notifyng them that you have seen their late to-do items. Productivity is always better with two!
